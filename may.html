When Hope Became A Dream

CYBER MUSE

May 27, 2025

I found myself asking questions

What does a butterfly see when

Its wings are torn apart from its body

Preventing it from flying, and being

Becoming now a husk of what it was

Will it shed a tear on what was?

Or will it understand that it's never been

And every step I take

Is one into the void she once spoke about

A never-ending dream on the edge

Of everything unspoken and unheard
